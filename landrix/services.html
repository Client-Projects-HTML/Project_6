Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our commercial maintenance partnerships.

How do you handle emergency snow removal?

Our contract clients have access to a 24/7 emergency line. We monitor weather patterns in real-time and dispatch crews automatically when accumulation hits your pre-defined threshold (usually 1-2 inches).

Are your services NFPA 96 compliant?

Yes, specifically for our commercial facility clients. We ensure all exterior maintenance around exhaust systems or kitchen waste areas meets strict safety standards.

Can I track multiple properties on one dashboard?

Absolutely. Our property manager portal is designed for high-volume portfolios, allowing you to filter by region, site status, or budget consumption across hundreds of locations.
