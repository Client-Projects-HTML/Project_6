Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our service agreements.

What is included in the seasonal contract?

Our standard seasonal contracts cover weekly maintenance (mowing/trimming) during the growing season and snow plowing whenever accumulation exceeds 1 inch during winter.

Can I cancel my service?

Yes, we offer flexible month-to-month options. Annual contracts can be cancelled with 30 days written notice, though early termination fees may apply.

How does billing work?

We bill on the 1st of each month for that month's services. We accept all major credit cards and ACH transfers via our client portal.

Do you offer one-time services?

Absolutely. While we specialize in ongoing maintenance, we're happy to provide quotes for one-off projects like landscape design, installation, or spring cleanups.
